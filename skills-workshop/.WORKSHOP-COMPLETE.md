## 💡 Workshop Delivery Tips

### Timing Recommendations
- **Morning (3 hours):** Chapters 1-2 + Lab 1
- **Afternoon (3 hours):** Chapters 3-5 + Labs 2-3
- **Wrap-up (30 min):** Chapter 6 + Q&A

### Key Teaching Points
1. **Progressive disclosure is critical** - Emphasize the 3-level system
2. **Description field determines triggering** - Spend extra time here
3. **Iterate quickly** - Encourage rapid prototyping
4. **Real use cases matter** - Use participants' actual workflows
5. **Testing prevents issues** - Don't skip testing exercises

### Common Questions to Prepare For
- "How do I know if my skill will trigger?"
- "Can I use multiple skills together?"
- "What if my skill is too long?"
- "How do I debug MCP issues?"
- "Can I share skills across my team?"
